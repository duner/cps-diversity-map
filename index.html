The Diversity of Chicago Public Schools

Each circle represents a public school in Chicago.
Color indicates the most prevalent group in each school —
  • ●White
  • ●Black
  • ●Hispanic
  • ●Asian
Circles are scaled relative to the size of the school's student body. Color transparency indicates the prevalence of the largest group.
Data from the Chicago Public Schools 1999/2000 and 2013-2014 School Year Racial/Ethnic Report.
